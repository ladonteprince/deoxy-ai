Beauty Science

The research behind the glow

Breakthrough papers proving that DNA-powered beauty isn't hype — it's science.

Nature 2023

First Topical Gene Therapy Approved

VYJUVEK: FDA approved the world's first gene therapy that can be painted onto skin. Uses engineered virus to deliver collagen genes. 65% wound closure vs 26% placebo. Opens the door to gene-level skincare.

Gene Therapy Collagen FDA Approved
ESDR 2024

Skin Cell Aging Reversed by 10+ Years

Turn Biotechnologies demonstrated mRNA-based epigenetic reprogramming that reversed aging in human skin cells by up to 10.4 years. First-ever rejuvenation of keratinocytes. Clinical trials planned for 2026.

Epigenetic Reversal mRNA Anti-Aging
Nature 2023

Gray Hair Is Reversible

Melanocyte stem cells get "stuck" in hair follicles — they don't die. This landmark Nature paper proved gray hair is caused by cell mobility failure, not cell death, opening the door to pigment restoration.

Hair Science Melanocytes Reversible
NEJM Evidence 2025

First Telomere-Lengthening Gene Therapy in Humans

EXG-34217 used AAV to deliver the ZSCAN4 gene, successfully lengthening telomeres in two patients over 24 months. First clinical proof that gene therapy can reverse telomere aging in humans.

Telomeres Gene Therapy Longevity
Obesity (Wiley) 2025

Your DNA Predicts Ozempic Response

GLP1R gene variant rs6923761 predicts weight loss on semaglutide. AA genotype lost weight at 1.64%/month vs 1.04% for G-carriers. Pharmacogenomics could save months of trial-and-error with GLP-1 drugs.

Pharmacogenomics GLP-1 Weight Loss
Aging 2025

Epigenetic Diet Reverses Age by 2+ Years

Specific dietary patterns rich in green tea, turmeric, rosemary, and berries were associated with a 2.04-year reduction in epigenetic age. You can literally eat your way to younger DNA methylation patterns.

Epigenetic Diet Methylation Nutrition
Nature Comms 2024

CRISPR Converts White Fat to Brown Fat

CRISPR-engineered human fat cells (HUMBLE) convert calorie-storing white fat into calorie-burning brown fat. Mice gained significantly less weight with 35% improved glucose tolerance. A pill version is in development.

CRISPR Metabolism Obesity
MD Anderson 2025

TAC: A Pill That Reactivates Telomerase

TAC (TERT Activating Compound) epigenetically de-represses the body's own telomerase gene. In aged preclinical models, 6 months of treatment led to new neuron formation and improved cognitive performance. Pill form.

Small Molecule Telomerase Oral
Cell Press 2024

mRNA Restores Elastin Production in Skin

Synthetic mRNA encoding tropoelastin triggered de novo elastin synthesis in human skin cells — the first demonstration of mRNA-mediated elastin production. Adults stop making elastin; this restarts it.

mRNA Elastin Skin Science
